How does Focus Troll work?

When you close a tab on distracting sites like Facebook, LinkedIn, or Twitter, Focus Troll automatically logs you out. The next time you try to visit, you'll need to log in again - just enough friction to break the endless scroll habit.

Is Focus Troll really free?

Yes! Focus Troll is completely free with no premium features. The troll works for everyone, no payment required.
