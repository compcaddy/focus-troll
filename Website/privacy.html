Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

Thank you for using Focus Troll, a browser extension designed to help you stay focused by automatically logging you out of distracting sites. Your privacy is important to us, and we're committed to being transparent about how we handle your information.

1. No Personal Data Collected

Focus Troll does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We believe in keeping your experience truly private — your usage, browsing habits, and logout history remain entirely on your device and in your browser.

2. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any form of analytics, cookies, tracking technologies, or third-party services that monitor your behavior inside or outside the extension. Your interactions with the extension are never recorded or transmitted.

3. No Account or Login Required

You can use the full functionality of the extension without creating an account or logging in. We do not require your email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.

4. Browser Permissions

Focus Troll requires permissions to access tabs and cookies on the sites you configure. These permissions are used solely to log you out when you close tabs on distracting sites. No data is sent to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

This extension is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk of storing information from children or adults.

6. Third-Party Services

Focus Troll does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs (such as analytics, ads, or cloud storage). Everything runs locally in your browser.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the extension or on the Chrome Web Store listing. Since we do not collect contact information, we will not send updates directly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us at: support@focustroll.com
